Donald Berry

One of, if not the most nonchalant person I know is myself, Donald Berry. It is no surprise after that statement that you should expect that this nonchalant attitude has been a great effect on my life and to a lesser extent my writing. The first piece of writing that I can remember doing was an essay in primary school, it was for our Boy’s Day activities. I remember working hard at it and I also remembered placing third in the essay competition. That is where my dislike for writing began. In high school I can recall that most of my writings were just the basic and only for a grade. I didn’t like writing.
